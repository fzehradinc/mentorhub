## 📐 Component Specifications

### Badge Card
- **Size**: 200×150px minimum
- **Border Radius**: 16px
- **Icon Size**: 32×32px
- **Hover Effect**: Scale 1.02, shadow increase
- **States**: Available, earned, locked

### Upload Areas
- **KYC Documents**: 320×200px drag zone
- **Company Logo**: 150×150px square
- **Progress Bar**: 8px height, rounded
- **File Preview**: 100×100px thumbnail

### Status Indicators
- **Pending**: Orange dot + "İnceleme aşamasında"
- **Processing**: Blue spinner + "İşleniyor..."
- **Approved**: Green checkmark + "Onaylandı"
- **Rejected**: Red X + "Reddedildi"

---

**Figma Export Sizes**:
- Mobile: 375×812px
- Tablet: 768×1024px
- Desktop: 1440×900px

**Component Library**: Badges, status cards, upload zones, progress bars
**Interaction States**: Default, hover, focus, loading, success, error
