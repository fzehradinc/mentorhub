## 📐 Component Specifications

### Status Toggle
- **Size**: 200×60px
- **States**: Draft, Published, Hidden
- **Animation**: Smooth slide transition
- **Colors**: Gray (draft), Green (published), Red (hidden)

### SEO Preview Cards
- **Google Snippet**: 400×120px
- **Social Cards**: 400×200px
- **Font**: Arial/Helvetica (Google style)
- **Colors**: Blue links, gray text

### Analytics Integration
- **Input Fields**: 300×44px
- **Status Indicators**: 16×16px circles
- **Integration Cards**: 200×150px
- **Active State**: Green border + checkmark

---

**Export Formats**: PNG, SVG, Figma Components
**Responsive Breakpoints**: 375px, 768px, 1024px, 1440px
**Accessibility**: WCAG AA compliant, keyboard navigation, screen reader support
